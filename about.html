WHO AM I?

Penny began her full-time tertiary study in 2015 with a Bachelor of Engineering at the University of New South Wales. She completed a Certificate IV in Industrial Design at the Sydney TAFE Design College. In 2017, Penny transferred to a Bachelor of Computational Design at UNSW and will graduate November 2019.

Penny is currently working casual as a junior design analyst for the NSW Department of Education in the Information Management Unit. Meta data and information must be communicated in a way that everyone at all levels can understand, thus, as a design analyst, information must be thoroughly understood in order to execute visuals in the most simplest form. Work such as presentation design and infographics are the primarily role.

One of Penny's hobbies is managing a business and sales. She currently owns a jewellery line Penchiicharms, as a sole trader, selling mainly handmade jewelleries. As a candidate of Ernst & Young Games Changer club, Penny has developed her knowledge in business and consulting in corporation with her work. In her spare time, Penny enjoys integrating her creativity and coding skills by creating an online interactive game to help HSC Chemistry students enhance their studies.
