Hello! I am Penny :)


Computational Designer focusing on interaction design with a passion for UX/UI and visual communication to deliver high-quality work in both aesthetic and functionality. I am based in Sydney and currently open for full-time work and graduation program.

MAIN SKILLS

VISUAL COMMUNICATION

Proficient in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign and After Effect, Penny is able to process complex information and represent them into a simple and clean graphics through different forms of media.

FRONT-END DEVELOPMENT

From research and analysing data with Python and JavaScript, Penny is able to take complex information and display with interactivity through web design and development with HTML, CSS, JavaScript using React/Redux web framework.

INTERACTION DESIGN

Using arduino and 3D parametric modelling tools such as Rhino3D and Grasshopper, Penny is able to design with empathy for the user to feel and sense the design in real-life.
