Interaction Design

Aquaractive Digital Water Curtain

Date: 18.11.2017
Tools: Adobe Photoshop, Rhino3D, Grasshopper, Keyshot Render, Arduino, Selenoid Valve
Members: Danielle Bisazza, Penny Talalak
Category: Urban Interaction Design

Aquaractive is a digital water curtain that spans across the front edge of the pool lawn.

The initial site analysis of the lawn can be found here


There are many problems with the lawn and making sure the lawn has a presence and activity within it is the first step to its rejuvenation. This is due to the entrance being inexplicit for users and hence there is minimal interactive between passers bys and the lawn. Thus, Aquaractive partakes in becoming the new interactive entrance to solve this.The feature is a multi-purposed solution, serving as a means of communication and passageway while simultaneously giving a playful and refreshing vibe, serving as an iconic landmark in the landscape.

Through this extravagant entrance more attention will be brought to the lawn, which is especially important with the new changes coming with the light rail, with stops near the lawn from the Squarehouse giving the opportunity for more passer bys.


The features interactive ability extends to the capability of using smartphones and laptops to digitally relay a pattern or text for the wall to display through a social media hashtag. Through doing this the social presence of the pool lawn will also dramatically increase.

Prototype

In attempt to realise this design concept we created a small prototype with features that would be included in the final design.

  • Series of values that are capable of opening and closing to allow for the flow of water
  • LEDs which illuminate the water for night time display
  • Pumping system which recycles water through the system - connected to a tap to adjust pressure
  • Ability to open up through detecting movement towards the curtain using ultrasonic sensor
  • Change patterns based on user input (through laptop 0 and 1) using a bluetooth module

Currently the pool lawn consists of 3 street lights for late night usage which does not illuminate the area very well. Aquarative will eliminate this issue, with interchangeable RGB LED lights during the night which guide late night users.
